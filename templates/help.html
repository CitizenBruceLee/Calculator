🚀 How to Use

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Select First Number: Click on the smiley faces in the "First Number" section to build your first number. You can use multiple smileys for larger numbers (e.g., 😊😀 = 10).
  2. Add Decimal Point: Click the "." button if you need decimal numbers (e.g., 😂.😄 = 3.5).
  3. Choose Operation: Click on one of the operation smileys (➕ ➖ ✖️ ➗) to select what you want to do.
  4. Select Second Number: Click on smiley faces in the "Second Number" section to build your second number.
  5. Calculate: Click the "🧮 Calculate" button to see your result!
  6. View Result: The result will appear in both smiley form and regular numbers for easy understanding.

💡 Tips & Tricks

  • 🎯 Multi-digit Numbers: Click multiple smileys to create bigger numbers (😊😁 = 12)
  • 🔄 Switch Numbers: Click on the number display boxes to switch between entering the first or second number
  • 🧹 Clear Numbers: Use the "Clear" button under each number to start over
  • 🗑️ Reset Everything: Click "Clear All" to start a completely new calculation
  • 💧 Decimals: Use the "." button to add decimal points to your numbers
  • 🎨 Visual Feedback: Selected operations are highlighted in green, active number input is highlighted in blue
